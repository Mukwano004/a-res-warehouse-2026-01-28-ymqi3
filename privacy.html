4.1 What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, to improve user experience, and to provide information to website owners about how their sites are used.

4.2 Types of cookies we may use

4.3 Managing cookies and preferences

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings, including blocking or deleting cookies. Please note that if you choose to disable certain cookies, parts of the Site may not function properly or your experience may be limited.

For more information about cookies and how to manage them, you may refer to your browser’s help documentation or independent resources that explain how cookies work.
